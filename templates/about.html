About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and team.

Our Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of machine learning to provide accurate predictions for epilepsy, helping individuals and healthcare providers make informed decisions. We strive to empower those affected by epilepsy with timely information and support.

Our Vision

We envision a world where epilepsy is understood and managed effectively, reducing the stigma and improving the quality of life for those affected. Our goal is to make epilepsy predictions accessible and reliable for everyone at risk.

Our Team

We are a passionate team of healthcare professionals, data scientists, and advocates dedicated to improving epilepsy care. Our diverse expertise allows us to approach challenges creatively and compassionately.

Community Support

We believe in the power of community. Through partnerships with organizations and awareness campaigns, we aim to provide resources and support to those impacted by epilepsy, fostering a supportive environment.
